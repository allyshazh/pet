About Us !

Petcare is a group of students from UiTM Arau, Perlis who created a website for their group project. The website that we created is one for Pet Hotel Booking System. It is useful for a pet hotel system to store their booking appointments, so that they could create a better system. This pet hotel is not only specified for cats but we provide this service for variety types of pet like cats, dogs, rabbits, hamsters and even birds ! You name it, we take it. Not only for the staffs, it is also useful for the customer to book their appointment, get to know more about the organization also for contact purposes.
